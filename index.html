About Us




Stories and Meals Cafe is a book cafe concept or a library-like cafe where we provide books for our customers to read while enjoying our freshly brewed coffee and fresh-from-the-oven pastries. Established on March 14, 2018, this café, which may be loved by book lovers, is located in the heart of Kuala Terengganu, near the iconic drawbridge, where our customers can also enjoy their readings and a cup coffee with a city view. We offered a wide range of books in every genre, from novels to motivational books and even children's tale books, to suit to our customers' reading preferences.

Furthermore, we also have a large variety of books by best-selling authors such as Teme Abdullah, Coleen Hoover, JK.Rowling and many others. our café employs professional baristas to prepare your coffee, and we utilise high-quality coffee beans that are 100 percent arabica coffee beans so you won't have to worry about the coffee quality. If you don't like coffee, we also serve fresh fruit juices and besides pastries, we also make a variety of other dishes that you can choose in our menu.
